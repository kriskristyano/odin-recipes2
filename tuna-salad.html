Tuna Salad

Home

Take a look at this creamy and tasty tuna salad! It's great to spread on bread as a sandwich, or eat by itself if you're watching the carbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Relish

Steps

  1. Drain tuna cans and put in a bowl.
  2. Add mayonnaise - adjust amount to creaminess preferences!
  3. Add a teaspoon each of salt and pepper
  4. Mix in relish to taste. Remember that more relish will result in a more watery consistency!
