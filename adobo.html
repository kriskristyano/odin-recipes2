Adobo

Home

Adobo is a Filipino meat dish, typically chicken or pork, simmered in a sauce made with vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices like bay leaves and peppercorns.

Ingredients

  • Pork or chicken
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Soy sauce
  • Bay leaves
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cut meat into bite-sized pieces and set aside in a large pot.
  2. Add vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, salt, and pepper into a bowl and mix well to create the sauce. Add half of the volume of the concoction of water in too.
  3. Combine sauce and meat in large pot.
  4. Bring to a boil and lower heat to a simmer, covered for 1 hour or longer until meat is tender.
  5. Serve hot over rice and enjoy!
