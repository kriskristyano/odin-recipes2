Chicken Thighs

Home

These juicy chicken thighs include very few ingredients but pack a lot of flavor! Not to mention the rendering the fat from the skin first makes some delicious crispy chicken skins to eat alongside it... Try it out!

Ingredients

  • Skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion powder

Steps

  1. Dry chicken thighs and debone them.
  2. Add garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper to the flesh side. Flip and only salt the skinned side.
  3. Place chicken thighs in a room temperature skillet skin-side down.
  4. Heat pan on medium heat long enough for the fat to render from the chicken skin. When skin is crispy, flip and fry until cooked thoroughly.
